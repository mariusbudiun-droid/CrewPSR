CrewPSR · Pescara Cabin Crew · v1.7.0 beta
🏠 Home — your shift today, flights and who can swap with you. Swipe left/right to browse the week.
📅 Calendar — tap any day to set your assignment (Aereo 1, Aereo 2, HSBY, AD)
✈️ Schedule — full flight timetable for every day of the week
🔄 Swap — find who can cover your shift
👥 Crew — add crew codes and WhatsApp numbers of colleagues
📊 Statistics — hours flown, top routes, airports and more
⚙️ Settings — change roster, cycle date, PIN
📱 Install on iPhone
  1. Open Safari → crew-psr-wyvq.vercel.app
  2. Tap Share (□↑) → "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap Add ✅
🤖 Install on Android
  1. Open Chrome → crew-psr-wyvq.vercel.app
  2. Tap ⋮ → "Add to Home Screen" → Install ✅
